Step 1. You must be logged in first.

Step 3. Select the check-in and check-out dates as well as the total number of guests. To see which villas are available at that moment, just click the search button.
Step 4. Any of the villas that are listed are available for reservation. Click the reserve button beside your chosen villa.
Step 5. You will be redirected to a page that summarizes the reservation's details. Simply click the "Confirm" button to add the cost of an additional breakfast or bed to the final bill.

Step 7. On the Booking Status page, wait for your reservation request to be approved. You may also proceed in processing the payment after being accepted.
