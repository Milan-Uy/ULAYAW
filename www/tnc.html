HOW CAN WE PROTECT THE DATA COLLECTED FROM YOU?

Our goal is to safeguard your personal data using a system of administrative and technical security controls. To ensure the protection of any personal information we process, we have put in place the necessary technical security measures. However, please keep in mind that neither the Internet nor the gadgets you use are 100% safe. Your personal information is transmitted to and from our servers at your own risk even though we will try to secure it.
